The Blugold Group

Who We Are

The Blugold Group is the Cybersecurity Club for the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire. We focus on providing hands-on, practical experiences which translate directly into the workforce.

We provide a welcoming environment where anyone of any skill level can learn cybersecurity skills.

We conduct research, compete at competitions, perform red teaming exercises, and foster learning through collaboration and hands-on workshops.
