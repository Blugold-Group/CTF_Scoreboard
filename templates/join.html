Why Join?

We regularly schedule workshops that explore a wide variety of topics within cybersecurity, including ethical hacking, penetration testing, and network security.

In addition, we also compete at CTF events and have guest speakers come and talk about their experience in the field.

Where and When?

We meet at 6:00pm in Vicki Lord Larson 1132 (in the hallway connecting Schofield to the library) every Tuesday. Come down and join us!

You can also check the calendar here.

FAQ

Do I need prior experience to join?

No! We welcome all skill levels. Workshops start with basics and build up.

Is membership free?

Yes! It's completely free and available for UW-Eau Claire students.

Who can I contact for questions?

Email us at cyberclub@uwec.edu.
