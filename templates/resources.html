Security Communities, Forums, and Blogs

  • Krebs on Security - A blog focused on the latest cybersecurity news, vulnerabilities, and breaches.
  • SANS Reading Room - A collection of white papers covering various aspects of cybersecurity.
  • r/cybersecurity - A subreddit where students and professionals discuss cybersecurity news and topics.
  • r/AskNetsec - A community for discussions related to ethical hacking and penetration testing.
  • r/networking - A subreddit dedicated to networking concepts and security discussions.

Security Courses

  • edX: Introduction to Networking and Security - A course introducing networking concepts and the basics of securing networks.
  • Cybrary: Introduction to IT and Cybersecurity - A free, foundational course covering a broad spectrum of cybersecurity concepts.
  • Coursera: Introduction to Cyber Security Specialization - A comprehensive introduction to cybersecurity topics.

  • OWASP Top 10 - A list of the most critical web application security risks, regularly updated.

  • Hack The Box - A penetration testing and ethical hacking platform with real-world challenges.
